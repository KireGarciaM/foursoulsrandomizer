Rules

Introduction

Below you will find the quick and easy guide on how to play in PDF booklet format. This has all the base rules of the game and how it functions. Although the booklet was introduced shortly after Requiem was released there have already been new keyterms and rules added to the game with the new booster packs. On top of that, this repo contains custom cards from various creators with custom mechanics and keywords added to the game to give it another flavor. After providing links to vanilla rulesets there will also be a new modded section that cover the custom cards on this site/repo.

Overview

Players take turns playing loot cards and activating abilities to kill monsters in order to gain more loot, items, and souls. The first player to control four souls is the winner. Cooperation, bartering, and betrayal are all strongly encouraged. Good luck!
