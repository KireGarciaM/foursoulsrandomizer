    Pokemon Expansion

    About

    This expansion contains cards with characters from pokemon. These were all handcrafted by me as an effort to expand upon Ezim's Familiars concepts introduced in his "Unknown Depths" Expansion. This expansion contains items to help power up pokemon you control and loot that helps catch and power up pokemon you have. This expansion also includes many characters from the games that you can play as (mostly taking inspiration from Pokemon Masters EX).     The Elder Scrolls Expansion

    About

    This expansion contains cards with characters from The Elder Scrolls Universe. These were all handcrafted by me as an effort to expand my skills on custom cards and add more companions introduced by Ezim in his "Four Souls: Evolved" Expansion. This expansion contains items and loot from the Elder Scrolls universe to help power up the player such as Sweetrolls and Mehrunes Dagger. This expansion also includes characters from the games that you can play as (Morrowind, Oblivion, and Skyrim) as well as monsters from the Elders Scrolls Universe such as Alduin, Dagoth Ur and Molag Bal. This is one of my ongoing projects so expect more loot, items and monsters to be added.
